Bag haikus for the extended voice-tree decomposition.

Bag C one prime — Sinitic enrichment.

Li Shanlan in Qing,
Changs curvature, Cherns forms,
centuries fold flat.

Bag D zero — Slavic nucleus.

Rivers born of snow,
equations beneath starlight,
silence asks the form.

Bag E — Slavic, Semitic, Persian bridge.

Three scripts at the door,
Cyrillic, Aleph, Alef,
each breath a new sun.

Bag F — Indo-Iranian.

Rhythm across mountains,
Devanagari meets Farsi,
prime numbers, whispered.

Bag G — Maritime South East Asia.

Islands speak in tones,
Thai, Vietnamese, Indonesian,
truth grows like new rain.

Bag X — cross-tree bridge.

Between two forests,
Lekha, Isha, Rishi stand,
a single shore rises.

End of bag haikus.
